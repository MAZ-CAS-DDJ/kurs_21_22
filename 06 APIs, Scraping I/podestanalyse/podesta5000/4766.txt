Click here for a sampling of his music (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5iTnrmRDE4U) , which embraces both Arabic and jazz styles.\n\nClassically trained on the Qanun, which originates from the ancient Egyptian harp, Paris has earned critical acclaim in the Middle East and abroad for more than a decade. His music crosses over from classical Arabic music to a variety of genres including Western and Middle Eastern jazz.\n\nJoining Paris on the stage will be local artists sharing their eclectic musical style.
